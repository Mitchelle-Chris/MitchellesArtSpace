About The Artist

I'm Mitchelle Chris and I'm just an artist who started drawing with no intent.

Drawing to me was some form of distraction but didn't have to go the extra mile to realise that I could draw, some part of me always knew I could create in some form or manner. But it's still been a journey to reach this point.

Favourite Brand

This one is a tough one. However, it would be Windsor and Newton!

Favorite Medium

Watercolours! But on a good day, I could never choose.

Profession or Hobby?

Art is, for the time being, only a hobby.
